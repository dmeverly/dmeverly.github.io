Background

I have practiced as an Adult-Gerontology Acute Care Nurse Practitioner in Emergency, Pulmonology, and Critical Care Medicine since 2017. I have proven skills in the management of critically-ill adults, but I strive to learn something new every day. I was originally a Computer Science major who transitioned to Nursing many years ago. Amidst the COVID pandemic, I was inspired by innovation all around me and wanted to develop new ways to help people on a broader scale. I revisited Computer Science and found a new passion in Artificial Intelligence. I am currently studying Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at Drexel University and have strong interest in projects that leverage the power of modern technology to improve healthcare quality, optimize workflows, and protect sensitive data.
